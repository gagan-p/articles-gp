## Open Questions (Do Not Force Resolution)

These questions are deliberately left open in the story. No chapter should
attempt to resolve them:

- Was the Kalinga War fought as described? (No archaeological confirmation)
- Is the copper disc the Kalinga Jina? (No — but the rhyme is there)
- Was Kalinga a merchant republic? (Labelled hypothesis, not argued)
- Was Vijaya from Ganjam? (Two stories that rhyme; rhyming is not evidence)
- What exactly did Pitambara write on 1 April 1936? (Revealed in Ch 15)
- Did Ashoka know the Kalinga Jina was in his treasury? (Both possibilities
  in the notebook; neither resolved)
